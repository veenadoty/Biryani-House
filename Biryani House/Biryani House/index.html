FAQ

What types of biryanis do you offer?

At Biryani House, we offer a variety of biryanis including traditional Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and South Indian biryanis. Each is crafted with authentic spices and the freshest ingredients. Check our menu for a complete list of options.

Are your biryanis available for delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery services to ensure you can enjoy our biryanis from the comfort of your home. Simply place your order through our website or app, and we’ll deliver it to your door.

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Our delivery service operates within these hours as well. Please check our website or contact us for any special holiday hours.

How can I track my delivery order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation with a tracking link. You can use this link to track the status of your delivery in real-time.

Are there any special offers or discounts available?

We regularly offer special promotions and discounts. Visit our website or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals and offers.
